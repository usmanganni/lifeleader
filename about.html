Why Choose us

Commercial, TVC and Video Productions in Singapore

Commercials and marketing projects often choose Singapore for its sleek and hip urban atmosphere. This ambiance translates very well to all formats and visual mediums, including film, high definition video, online content, and broadcast television. With brand new, glistening high rise architecture contrasting old-world colonial buildings, Singapore simply delivers. The choices are seemingly endless, and any director or producer will marvel at the options this city has to offer.

Imaginary Cloud Story

With an amazing city vibe, Singapore awakens the senses and brings any cinema project to life with energetic, crackling scenery. Blessed with cultural diversity, there are a host of location options all within reach. These include pedestrian alleyways, rooftops, five-star hotels and rustic open-air markets where brusque hawkers ply their trade. The list goes on. Singapore doubles well for anywhere from China to India and even in some areas can supplement for frames of Manhattan and New York. This morphing, dynamic environment provides endless opportunity to directors and cinematographers with an eye on producing exceptional quality footage. Visiting foreign productions have always been satisfied with Singapore’s local hires and logistics support. These consummate professionals can fulfill any film’s needs including location scouting, art department, prop houses and operation of top of the line editing suites.
